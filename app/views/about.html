About

WeatherTop Inc. produce a modular weather station called the WeatherTop1000. This low-cost device takes a set of meteorological readings and displays these on a simple LCD display. This application performs calculations on the data and provides an easy to read display of all recorded information. Members of the app are also able to add and delete, as many stations and readings as they like.
